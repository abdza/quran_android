## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Tadabbur is a Quran study app designed to help you understand the Quran word by word. The name "Tadabbur" comes from the Quranic concept of deep reflection and contemplation of Allah's words.

**Word-by-Word Learning**
• See each Arabic word with its meaning
• Customizable Arabic and translation text sizes
• Transliteration support for pronunciation
• Full ayah translations from multiple scholars

**Memorization Mode**
• Hide Arabic or translation to test yourself
• Adjustable reveal delay
• Perfect for hifz practice

**Beautiful Quran Reading**
• High-quality Madani mushaf pages
• Night mode for comfortable reading
• Bookmark your favorite ayahs
• Quick navigation by surah, juz, or page

**Audio Recitation**
• Listen to renowned Qaris
• Stream or download for offline use
• Follow along with highlighted ayahs

**Multiple Translations**
• Download translations in many languages
• Compare multiple translations side by side
• Show Arabic text alongside translation

**Features**
• Completely free, no ads
• Offline reading after initial download
• Search the entire Quran
• Share ayahs with friends and family
• Lightweight and fast

Tadabbur is open source and based on the Quran Android project. We respect your privacy - no tracking or analytics.

May this app help you in your journey to understand and reflect upon the Holy Quran.
