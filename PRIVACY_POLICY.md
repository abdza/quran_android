# Privacy Policy for Tadabbur

**Last updated: February 2, 2026**

## Overview

Tadabbur is a Quran reading and study application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Usage analytics (when built without Firebase)

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
- Bookmarks and reading progress
- App preferences and settings
- Downloaded translations and audio files

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

### Network Requests

The app connects to the internet only to:
- Download Quran page images
- Download translations
- Download and stream audio recitations

These downloads are from quran.com servers and are necessary for the app to function.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your bookmarks, reading history, or preferences.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact
